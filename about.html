About Us

    Volta is a pioneering and innovative company located in Sri Lanka. Our company specializes in designing and manufacturing smart holders for light bulbs, providing efficient and innovative lighting solutions to our customers. Our products are designed with cutting-edge technology that makes them energy-efficient, eco-friendly, and easy to use. Our company has a team of engineering undergraduates who work tirelessly to create products that cater to the evolving needs of our customers.

   We are committed to providing the best products and services.We are also dedicated to being environmentally conscious and reducing our carbon footprint by designing products that are eco-friendly and sustainable. With our smart holders, we strive to make a positive impact on our customer's lives by providing them with innovative and convenient lighting solutions that are easy to use, energy-efficient, and sustainable.

Our Team

  Our team comprises three talented undergraduates from the University of Peradeniya's Engineering Faculty in their third year of studies. Each member of our team brings unique skills and expertise to the table, including experience in electrical and electronic engineering, programming, and project management. We are passionate about creating innovative products that can make a positive impact on people's lives, and we strive to deliver top-quality products that exceed our customers' expectations.

  Our team works collaboratively,bringing together different perspectives and ideas to develop the best solutions possible. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and our commitment to providing excellent customer service. At the heart of our team's work is a dedication to using our knowledge and skills to create products that are functional, reliable, and easy to use.
