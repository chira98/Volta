Smart Holder Features

Rechargable

    This feature ensures that you have continuous lighting even during unexpected power outages,giving you peace of mind and reliable uninterrupted lighting. Our rechargeable feature is designed to detect power outages and automatically switch to the battery mode, providing uninterrupted lighting until the power is restored. Once the power is back on, the rechargeable battery automatically starts to recharge, so it's always ready for the next outage.

   Our product is perfect for those who live in areas prone to power outages, as it offers a reliable lighting solution that you can count on.With our rechargeable feature, you don't need to worry about finding alternative sources of lighting or fumbling around in the dark during power outages.

Smart

   You can turn on and off your light bulb using your mobile phone, laptop, or any browser. This means that you can control your lighting from anywhere in the home, at any time, making it easy, energy saving by turning off lights when you're not using them.

Easy Installtion

   Our smart holder products are designed to make installation easy and hassle-free. Our product can be connected to the existing holder in your home, which means that you do not need any additional wiring or electrical work. This makes our product an ideal choice for those who want a smart lighting solution without the need for costly and time-consuming installation work.

   Our easy installation process involves simply screwing in the smart holder to the existing holder, and it's ready to use. The design of our smart holder also ensures that it is compatible with all types of light bulbs, giving you the flexibility to use the bulb of your choice. With our easy installation process, you can have a smart lighting solution in your home in just a matter of minutes, without any need for additional wiring or electrical work.

User Friendly

   We prioritize the user experience and strive to create products that are user-friendly and accessible to everyone. Our smart holders are designed with intuitive controls that can be easily used by anyone, from children to the elderly. Our browser application is user-friendly and has a simple interface, making it easy to control your lighting with just a few taps on your smartphone,tablet or Laptop.

   In addition to our mobile app, our smart holders can also be controlled using a manual wall switch, which is familiar and easy to use for anyone. The manual switch is designed with intuitive controls that allow you to turn your lights on and off with ease.

   We believe that technology should be accessible to everyone, and our user-friendly design ensures that our smart holders can be used by anyone, regardless of their level of technological expertise.
