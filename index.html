Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a smart holder and how does it work?

    A smart holder is an innovative lighting holder solution that allows you to control your lights using your mobile phone, laptop or tablet. It works by connecting the smart holder to the existing holder in your home.

  • What type of light bulbs are compatible with the smart holder?

    Our smart holder is designed to be compatible with all types of light bulbs, including LED, CFL bulbs.

  • Do I need any special equipment or wiring to install the smart holder?

    No, you do not need any special equipment or wiring to install the smart holder. Our product can be connected to the existing holder in your home, making installation quick and easy.

  • Do I need my manual Switch to turn on?

    Yes, you have to keep it turn on and can be controlled using the mobile app and browser. This provides you with multiple options for controlling your lights and makes the product more accessible to all users.

  • What type of warranty is included with the smart holder?

    Our product comes with a standard one-year warranty for any defects or malfunctions.

  • Can I control multiple smart holders with the same URL?

    Yes, you can control multiple smart holders with the same URL but you have to connect to the each holder Access Points. This allows you to create a customized lighting setup for your entire home.
